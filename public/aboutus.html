         About Us

This app has been created by three students at the University of California Santa Barbara in conjuction with ECE594BB(special topics in embeded systems). We have created this application to demonstrate computing possibilities and limitations on mobile devices and to show an example of using real time facial recognition, something that can become much more useful in the future as hardware improves.
